Watching Series, Anime & Movies

ANIME

Anime is one of my favorite forms of entertainment because it combines strong storytelling, emotional depth, and unique visual styles.

My Hero Academia — A shōnen anime about Izuku Midoriya training at U.A. High School.

Haikyuu!! — A sports anime about high-school volleyball teams.

Demon Slayer — Dark fantasy anime about Tanjiro joining the Demon Slayer Corps.

The Apothecary Diaries — Historical mystery anime set in the imperial palace.

Hell’s Paradise — Criminals sent to a deadly island seeking immortality.

Ron Kamonohashi’s Forbidden Deductions — Mystery comedy with a genius detective.

Buddy Daddies — Two hitmen unexpectedly raising a child.

MOVIES

Movies tell powerful stories in a shorter but emotional format.

Maleficent — Dark fantasy retelling of Sleeping Beauty.

Wicked — Musical fantasy exploring Elphaba and Glinda.

The Paradise of Thorns — Thai drama about loss and reclaiming home.

SERIES

Series allow long-term character development and deeper emotional connection.

The Empress of Ayodhaya — Thai historical palace drama.

Wednesday — Supernatural mystery at Nevermore Academy.

The Watcher — Thriller about threatening letters.

The Believers — Thai crime drama involving a risky temple scam.

Tale(s) of the Nine Tailed — Korean urban fantasy romance.

The Wicked Game — Thai drama full of manipulation.

Don’t Come Home — Horror series with paranormal events.

I Love ‘A Lot Of’ You — Thai slice-of-life romance.
